Bike tours in Uttarakhand
Overview
Uttarakhand has a very suitable place for riders. The Crookes roads, steep climb, dark turns, terrifying slops of Uttarakhand Himalaya are ready to take the tough test of the riders.

Uttarakhand is a delightful state with beautiful valleys, sky – high mountains, rivers, large meadows spread over miles, sacred temples and cold desert is sure to enchant the riders. And The Adventure Journey organizes such kind of bike tour where one can cover most the beautiful places of Uttarakhand.

In addition, we design itinerary as per the convenience of the tourists. So that the tourist can move around according to there choice.

We provide all kind of bikes on rental basis according rider’s demand.

Itinerary
Day 01 Arrival – Rishikesh
Day 02 Morning after rafting trip to Pauri – 110 kms.
Day 03 Pauri – Soneprayag - 138 kms.
Day 04 Sonepryag to Gaurikund 5 kms. Drive and further 18 kms. Trek. To Kedarnath.
Day 05 Kedarnath – 18 kms.trek down Gaurikund and 70 kms. Drive to Chopta.
Day 06 Chopta to Auli 148 kms. Ride.
Day 07 Auli – Gorson 3-4 kms. Trek – Auli – 38 kms drive – Lata village.
Day 08 Lata village – Niti village (last village on Tibet order) – Lata – 126 km. (to & fro)
Day 09 Lata village – Badrinath – Mana – 67 km. and will take 2 ½ hours.
Day 10 Badrinath - Rudraprayag – 155 km. and takes 5 hours.
Day 11 Rudraprayag – Rafting camp Marine drive (Rishikesh) – Rafting.
Day 12 Breakfast in camp and departure According to time scheduled for your return flight/train, drop at Airport/railway station.
